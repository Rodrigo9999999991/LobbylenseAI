  • Why Our Team Remains Anonymous

    At LobbyLens AI, our mission is to bring transparency to the financial influence of public officials and their networks. Ironically, in order to safely build this kind of transparency-focused tool, our team must remain anonymous.

    Here's why:

    1. Political Sensitivity

    LobbyLens AI analyzes and publicly exposes financial patterns and stock trades linked to politicians, lobbyists, and other high-profile individuals. This inherently challenges powerful institutions. Maintaining anonymity protects our team from political, legal, or retaliatory pressure that could compromise the project's independence or safety.

    2. Freedom to Build Without Interference

    We aim to operate in a neutral, data-driven way. By staying pseudonymous, we can build without being subject to identity-based bias, character attacks, or lobbying attempts that could influence the direction of the platform.

    3. Cybersecurity & Doxxing Risks

    The nature of this work attracts unwanted attention, especially from actors with resources and vested interests. Revealing our identities could lead to doxxing, threats, and personal or digital attacks that endanger the safety of our developers and the integrity of the platform.
